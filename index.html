Career & Tech Education

Providing students with hands-on, industry-certified skills for the workforce of tomorrow.

Advanced Woodshop

Master the craft of woodworking, from basic hand tools to advanced CNC machinery. Students build custom furniture and gain OSHA certifications.

Explore Program →

Software Engineering

Learn to build the future. This program covers Python, JavaScript, and web frameworks, culminating in an industry-recognized AP CS certification.
